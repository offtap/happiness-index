About the Happiness Index

We built this site to provide useful and elegant information, available for public use. The data is census data provided from the Australian Bureau of statistics.

Made by some kids at Bond

  1. Hello

    Welcome to the 2014 Australian Happiness Index! Search and compare locations around Australia based on census data and find out more about the places that mean the most to you.

  2. Search

    Type in a local government area, then press submit! A bar graph will display data for your chosen location below.

  3. Compare

    Enter another local government area to compare against.

  4. Find out more!

    Once you submit and compare, you can click on the different sections of the bar graph to find out more data about your chosen locations.

  5. Map

    Browse the map using the legend below to compare locations. How does your area match up?
